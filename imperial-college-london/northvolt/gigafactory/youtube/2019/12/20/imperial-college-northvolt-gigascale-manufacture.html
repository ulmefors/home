On 10 December 2019 I was invited to Imperial College London to give a talk on Northvolt’s progress so far, and what’s in store for the future. The title was Giga-Scale European Li-ion Manufacture: Bold bets, deep analytics, sustainable by design. The session was jointly hosted by Energy Futures Lab and Dyson School of Design Engineering.
